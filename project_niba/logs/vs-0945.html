8 July 2008
A new subject has been brought in today. Our goal with this subject,
Subject VS-0945, is to increase their visual awareness. VS-0945 is an
eight year-old female with prescription glasses to help with their
visual impairment. After the last five failed experiments and
subsequent terminations of subjects HR-1438 and VS-0944, Mr.
[REDACTED] is threatening our department with discontinuation if the
next few experiments fail. To be completely honest, I don’t know if
these new compounds inside the NIBAD, the Non-Invasive Brain Altering
Device, are working, but I can’t really voice my opinion without the
risk of being removed from the project. As the head caretaker of
VS-0945, I’ll leave an update here when any new information comes to
light.

5 August 2008
Subject VS-0945 has been undergoing pre-experiment analysis and has
spent quite a lot of time in recreational room 2A. I’ve noticed that
they’ve grown quite attached to Subject PL-0002, and they have told
me on multiple occasions that they have become friends. PL-0002 has
always been reclusive ever since they arrived here, so I think it’s
nice that they and VS-0945 have grown together. The only problem is
that PL-0002 is beginning experimentation tomorrow. I just hope
nothing bad happens.

7 August 2008
PL-0002 didn’t survive the experiment. A severe hemorrhage caused by
the experimental compounds administered by the NIBAD led to brain
death only twenty minutes after the hemorrhage was spotted by
Assistant Researcher Andrews. I’ve been panicking for the past two
hours... What do I tell VS-0945? They’ve been so happy talking to
PL-0002, what will they do without them?... I might have an idea.

19 August 2008
I’m so morally conflicted right now. My idea was to tell VS-0945 that
PL-0002 was still undergoing experiments and to give them a picture
of PL-0002 to talk to. They bought it a little too well. They haven’t
left their room in a week. They haven’t talked to anyone else but
that picture and me. It’s weighing on me mentally. What do I do?

22 August 2008
VS-0945 has been asking me about PL-0002 for the past few days now.
I’ve just kept telling them that they are undergoing experiments, but
I don’t know how long I can keep this going until I break. VS-0945
will go into their experiment tomorrow. I hope that keeps me
distracted, at least for a little bit.

24 August 2008
I can hear their screams through the walls. I don’t know if the
compounds are working; the researchers won’t tell me anything. You
know, in all the time that I’ve been on this program, I’ve never
grown attached to any subjects. But this one… this one’s different.
I’m going to pause these logs for a while. I need a break.

27 August 2008
I’m coming back here to describe an accident that occurred the other
day during VS-0945’s experiment. The NIBAD misfired while the
researchers were... assisting VS-0945 into the restraints and its beam
entered the subject’s frontal lobe. Due to the setting currently set,
her frontal lobe was shrunk. I’ve almost immediately noticed a change
in their behavior. Beforehand, VS-0945 was a bright young girl, but
now she acts more like a rebellious teenager. If I notice anything
else, I will update this log.

1 September 2008
VS-0945’s experiment has come to a close. Luckily, besides the
accident described previously, no other incidents have occurred. I
will continue to monitor VS-0945 for any changes. On a different
note, VS-0945 has continued to talk to the picture of PL-0002. She
still asks me about PL-0002. I think I’m going to have to tell her
soon.

4 September 2008
I’ve been having terrible nightmares recently. My husband has had to
shake me awake because I’m yelling and pleading in my sleep for help.
PL-0002 keeps occupying my mind. VS-0945 keeps occupying my mind. I’m
going to tell her tomorrow.

5 September 2008
I did it. I told her. She just stared at me in silence until I
quickly left. I couldn’t bear to see the tears forming in her eyes. I
don’t know if I feel better or worse.

6 September 2008
I finally got a good night’s sleep for the first time in a while when
I was woken up by a phone call at 2 in the morning. It was the lab.
VS-0945 was gone. There was no sign of escape or tampering, but the
cameras in her room were disabled. All of our security doors were
open, as if someone had just waltzed right through our building and
left. Did I cause this? What have I done? I should’ve never gotten
attached to her.

8 September 2008
I don’t know why, but ever since last Saturday, people have been
looking at me as if they had seen a ghost. It’s like I’m not meant to
be here. All that is happening is that I’m being transferred to
another department. A bit unfortunate, but I understand why the UDE
is making that happen. I’ll finish out this log in a few days.

9 September 2008
Someone broke into my house last night. I awoke to the sound of
shattering glass downstairs. I had to shake my husband awake, since
he didn't wake up from the sound. We both slowly exited our bedroom and
flicked on the stairway light. I went downstairs first and flicked on
all of the lights I could. No one was in the house. All I found was
a broken kitchen window and an open back door. The intruder must
have left when they heard us wake up. Who could it have been?

12 September 2008
Someone broke in again. What is going on? We're going to have to spend
all of our savings on window repairs...

13 September 2008
Again. What is happening?

14 September 2008
I'm staying up later now. I'm trying to see if I can catch whoever is
doing this. I will find out.

28 September 2008
I've been hearing things that aren't there. I've been seeing things
that aren't there. I can't sleep. I can't sleep.
